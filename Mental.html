Mental Health Support

We provide various services to support mental health, including one-on-one counseling, peer support groups, and stress management workshops.

Anxiety

Anxiety is a feeling of worry, nervousness, or unease. It's often characterized by excessive fear or stress.

Stress

Stress is a physical, mental, or emotional strain or tension. It can be caused by challenging situations or demands.

Depression

Depression is a mood disorder that causes persistent feelings of sadness and loss of interest. It can affect daily life and well-being.
